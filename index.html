About us

Everything began in 2020 when me and my elder cousin sister decided to open a pure bengali restaurant.We opened the restaurant in 2020 at Siliguri. Today, ‘Petuk Mon’ can be found in 3 cities nationwide. Furthermore, presently it’s additionally in Siliguri. At ‘Petuk Mon’ Siliguri, you can appreciate a wide determination of mutton biriyani, rosogolla, chicken roll and more luscious dishes, that will have you sipping’ and slurping and gorgin’ and more.
